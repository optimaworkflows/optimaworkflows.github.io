We compare your past freight invoices against your carrier contract rates, at no cost. If we find no overcharges, you owe nothing. If we do, you deduct it from the carrier bill before paying — we take 40% of that amount, you keep 60%.

We audit your most recent 30 days of retailer deductions — the window that matters. Amazon allows 30 days to dispute, Walmart 15–30 days from posting, Target just 14 days for compliance chargebacks. Common Questions

What exactly do you need from us to start a trial?

For the audit services (vendor invoices, freight, chargebacks), we need the relevant historical records — invoices, receiving logs, freight bills, or remittance/chargeback data — for the period we're auditing. For appointment booking, we need your list of old, uncontacted leads and access to send/receive on your own phone number, granted through a scoped, revocable key — never your account password.

What happens to our data after the trial?

Your files are used only to run the audit or campaign you requested and aren't shared or reused for anything else. We're happy to sign a simple data-handling agreement before you send anything sensitive.

Is there really no cost if you don't find anything?

Correct. Invoice and freight audits are paid as a share of what you deduct before payment — if we find nothing, there's nothing to pay. Chargeback recovery and appointment booking are paid on results actually delivered — if we book or recover nothing, you owe nothing.

Is the SMS/calling outreach compliant?

We follow TCPA consent and Do-Not-Call requirements on every campaign, including a DNC scrub before your leads are contacted. If your lead source's original opt-in doesn't cover the outreach channel we'd use, we'll flag that before starting rather than after.
