What You Need to Know Before You Proceed

What does the audit actually check for?

We compare what you were invoiced against what you ordered and received — unit prices against contracted rates, billed quantities against delivered quantities, and duplicate or double-billed line items.

What records do you need?

Vendor invoices, purchase orders, and receiving/goods-received records for the period audited — typically the last 60 days, exported as CSV or Excel from your ERP or accounting system.

How do you get paid?

40% of what we help you recover. If nothing recoverable is found, there's no fee at all.

Is my financial data safe?

Yes — your files are used only for the audit you requested and are never sold, shared, or reused. We provide a data-handling agreement before anything sensitive is sent.
