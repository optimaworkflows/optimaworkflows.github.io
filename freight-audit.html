What We Actually Check

Accessorial charge matching

Every accessorial line item — detention, liftgate, residential delivery, redelivery — checked against your contract's actual agreed terms.

Fuel surcharge verification

Fuel surcharges recalculated against the index your contract specifies, catching stale or incorrect rate application.

Wait-time and detention penalty review

Charges cross-checked against proof-of-delivery timestamps to catch penalties billed for delays that weren't your fault.

A documented findings report

Every discrepancy backed by the source invoice, contract clause, and delivery record — ready to dispute directly with the carrier.
